Baseline Placement Rules Summary

Rule for VR Stereoscopic Video

Keep the cameras exactly 60 to 65 mm apart (matching human eyes). Mount them perfectly parallel. Do not tilt them inward. This ensures the output recording feels correct when played in a VR headset.

Rule for 3D Scanning / Reconstruction

Choose a baseline roughly 10% to 20% of the camera-to-object distance. For scanning small desktop items 30 cm away, a baseline of 3 to 6 cm is optimal. Keep them slightly toed-in if maximizing close overlap is required.
